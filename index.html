Get to Know Our Teachers

Hanan - Basic Subjects Teacher

Ms. Hanan is responsible for your child's learning journey in class, where she teaches essential subjects such as Science, Math, Islamic Studies, and Storytelling. She fosters curiosity and creativity in her students through engaging lessons, hands-on activities, and interactive storytelling sessions to develop kids' love for learning.

Sarah - Arts & Music Teacher

Mrs. Sarah inspires creativity through arts, music, and dance. She nurtures children's artistic talents while encouraging them to express themselves through rhythm, and color, making the classroom a happy space for all.

Sana - Outdoor P.E. Teacher

Ms Sana believes physical activity is key to good health and that outdoor play is a fun way for kids to learn teamwork and stay active.
